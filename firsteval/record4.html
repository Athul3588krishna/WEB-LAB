Indian Festivals

Onam

Onam is Kerala's biggest ten-day harvest festival, celebrating the homecoming of King Mahabali and the bounty of the rice harvest. Celebrated in the Malayalam month of Chingam (August–September), festivities include decorating homes with flower carpets (pookalam), grand feasts (Onasadya), Pulikali (tiger dance) and Vallamkali (boat races). It is a festival of unity and joy in Kerala.

Diwali

The five days of Diwali — Dhanteras, Naraka Chaturdashi, Lakshmi Puja, Govardhan Puja, and Bhai Dooj — each have special meaning. Diwali, the Festival of Lights, marks the victory of good over evil and light over darkness.

Christmas

Christmas is celebrated on December 25 to commemorate the birth of Jesus Christ. It is marked with prayers, decorations, carols, gifts, and joyful gatherings across the world.
