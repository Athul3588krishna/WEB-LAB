About Us



MEA Engineering College, the first NAAC-accredited self-financing engineering college in Malappuram district, is committed to providing excellent and value-based education with a flair for ethics and professionalism. The college is located amidst panoramic natural beauty overlooking the hills and valleys at Nellikunnu, near Perinthalmanna – a city known for its educational institutions. It is governed by the Muslim Educational Association (MEA) under the “Samastha Kerala Jammiyyathul Ulama.” The institution is affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and approved by AICTE.

Courses Offered

MEA Engineering College offers B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA, and Ph.D. programs across multiple engineering and technology disciplines. Undergraduate programs focus on fundamental engineering branches, while postgraduate programs specialize in advanced technical areas.
