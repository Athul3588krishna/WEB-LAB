Weather seasons in india

Weather seasons are periods of the year with distinct and relatively consistent weather patterns, driven by Earth's tilt and position relative to the Sun, resulting in varied temperatures and day lengths. The most common four seasons are winter, spring, summer, and autumn (or fall).
In India and other tropical regions, seasons are often defined differently, with main seasons like winter, summer, and monsoon being prominent, and some areas also recognizing a post-monsoon or a dry season.
